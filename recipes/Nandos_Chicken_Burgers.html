Nandos Chicken Burgers

Delicious Nandos Chicken Burgers

A recipe that will delight your taste buds like your in Nandos!

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Brioche Buns
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Nandos Bag
  • Garlic paste

Optional:

  • Spicy Rice
  • Oven Chips
  • Corn

Steps

  1. Place the chicken breasts in the nandos bag & cook them in the oven at 200 for 30 Mins
